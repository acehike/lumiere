DESCRIPTION

Located at the heart of the Central Business District, Lumiere is a 45-storey residential building comprising a total of 168 apartments and four shops on the first level. Facilities include a gym, a lap pool, a tennis court and a basketball half court. Residents can also enjoy spectacular views from the sky garden located on the 34th storey.
